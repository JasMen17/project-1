WHO ARE WE?

We are a nonprofit designed to help and build a community of immigrants within the University of Houston School System. From students to workers, we believe everybody deserves the right to work towards a better future.

President: Armador Izquierdo

Vice president: Deysi Palmas

Outreach manager: Chuck Maxwell

Armando is the founder and president of this resource driven outreach. His only goal began with a need to unite and inform the students within the university of Houston about their rights to work under DACA.

Deysi is a strong activist and leader in the community spending several decades coordinating with the staff and their education departments within the University of Houston to spread awareness of employment rights.

Chuck has spent several years making an effort in the Hispanic/latine community when it comes to uniting student workers inside of the university of Houston as well as the surrounding community.
